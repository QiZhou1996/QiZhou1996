## Hi there 👋
![My Career Journey](https://github.com/QiZhou1996/a-career-journey-timeline-project/blob/main/Vizzes/Viz.png)

I am now building a ten-year career in data and AI, leveraging my previous journey as follows:

(2023 - 2025)
To develop a deep understanding of the world and foster creativity, I took a career gap from August 2023, during which I achieved two Google certificates in data analytics and completed four self-directed analysis projects. Additionally, I gained new perspectives through two self-planned three-week international trips plus continuous reading.

(2020 - 2023)
To acquire versatile and efficient problem-solving skills, I was employed as a technical support engineer for Microsoft Azure services, which honed my experience in providing customer service to international clients and my ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams.

(2019 - 2020)
To further fuel my intellectual curiosity about science and technology, as well as diverse cultures and languages, I travelled from China to London to study a master's programme and earned a Master of Science degree in Artificial Intelligence from King's College London.

Looking forward to bringing this blend of technical rigour and human-centric problem-solving to a team that values data-driven growth as much as they value the people behind the numbers.
